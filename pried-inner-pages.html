The People Behind PRIED

Meet Our Team

A multidisciplinary team of researchers, journalists, advocates, and policy experts united by the conviction that evidence-based research can drive a just and sustainable energy future for Pakistan.

B
Chief Executive Officer

Muhammad Badar Alam

Muhammad Badar Alam started his professional career as a journalist in 1992. He worked on senior editorial positions with leading news organizations including The News International and Pakistan's premium English monthly Herald, which he edited from 2010 until its forced closure in July 2019. In April 2020, he co-founded loksujag.com, a news website focused on amplifying voices from the margins of power. He has also served as Executive Director of the Rural Development Policy Institute (RDPI) and a Director at the Sustainable Development Policy Institute (SDPI).

Director & Project Lead

Azhar Ali Lashari

With an academic background in mass communication and anthropology, Azhar has spent more than two decades working with national and international NGOs on environmental campaigns related to land, water, and forest in Pakistan. At PRIED, he leads advocacy work on social, economic, and environmental issues in the energy sector.

Naila works primarily on decentralised renewable energy, green finance, and socio-technical transition. She holds a PhD from TU Berlin in economics and management. Manager Finance and HR

Muhammad Kashif
