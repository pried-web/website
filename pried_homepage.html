What We Do

Four Pillars of Our Work

PRIED operates across research, collaboration, advocacy, and outreach to accelerate Pakistan's just energy transition.

🔬
Research

We conduct rigorous, evidence-based research on climate justice, energy transition, and equitable development — producing actionable insights for policymakers and practitioners across Pakistan and beyond.

Read More
🤝
Collaboration

We build strategic partnerships with governments, civil society, international organizations, and research institutions to co-create solutions that are inclusive, scalable, and locally relevant.

Read More
📢
Advocacy

We translate research into policy recommendations and engage decision-makers at national and international levels to advance equitable, climate-responsive legislation and governance frameworks.

Read More
🌱
Outreach

We engage communities, media, and civil society to build awareness and ownership of the energy transition narrative — ensuring that those most affected are at the centre of change.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

To accelerate Pakistan's transition from fossil fuels to a clean, affordable, and just energy system — by generating evidence, informing policy, and amplifying the voices of communities most impacted by the climate crisis. We believe that equitable development and climate action are not competing goals but mutually reinforcing imperatives.
